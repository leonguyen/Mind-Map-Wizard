2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

2.2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with Mind Map Wizard, such as the features you use, the date and time of your visits, and other similar details.
  • Device and Connection Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access Mind Map Wizard (e.g., IP address, operating system, browser type, device identifiers).
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your usage of Mind Map Wizard and to provide a more personalized experience.

2.2.1. Local Data Storage and Retention

Your mind maps and interaction data are primarily stored locally in your browser. Please note:

  • You can delete all recent mind maps at once using the clear history button on Mind Map Wizard or by clearing your browser data.
  • Recent mind maps are automatically deleted after reaching 100 mind maps.
  • Different browsers handle data retention differently - some may automatically delete your data if you haven't accessed Mind Map Wizard for an extended period, while others may retain it indefinitely unless manually cleared.

2.2.2. Data Anonymization Practices

We are committed to protecting your privacy. To ensure this, Cloudflare implements the following data anonymization measures before sending any data to Google Analytics:

  • URL Query Parameter Removal: We automatically remove all query parameters from URLs (e.g., converting https://example.com/?q=mind-map-topic to https://example.com/) to prevent collection of potentially sensitive search or form data.
  • IP Address Trimming: We trim IP addresses before passing them to server-side loaded tools, reducing the precision of location data while maintaining basic analytics functionality.
  • User Agent String Cleaning: We clean sensitive information from User Agent strings by removing versions, extensions, and additional information that could be used for device fingerprinting.
